Addington Acres Branding

Completed 2018

This is both a personal and school project. For my major studio project, I decided I would do another branding project, instead this time for a fictional family farm. I got the inspiration from my love of farmers markets. I wanted a challenge in adapting a traditional industry to modern design.

This project highlights my diversity of skills as it includes a functioning website, a video, logo and stylesheet, product packaging, promotional material and social media marketing - all done by myself. Although it is not yet fully complete, this project is teaching me how to use my diverse array of skills to create a complete, functioning brand that could be used in the real world.
